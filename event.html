What is Cardistry-Con?

"Cardistry-Con is an interactive conference centered around the art of cardistry, where
cardists from all over the world can gather and explore the limitless expressive
the potential of an ordinary deck of playing cards. We promote cardistry in an encouraging
environment suitable for anyone passionate about the art"
- Cardistry-Con official website.
