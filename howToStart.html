How to Start

To Start Cardistry you only need one thing PLAYING CARDS!
We recommend a deck with air cushion technology, for your
convenience we have gathered the list of our recommended
playing cards!

Bicycle Playing Cards

This is one of the most popular brand not only for magicians but
also poker in general. manufactured by USPCC(United States
Playing Card Company).

This is a really good starter choice with an affordable price. most
cardist start with this card

Bee Playing Cards

Another playing card that is manufactured by USPCC.
Top-notch quality with an incredibly mesmerizing back design.
This is another famous choice for starters as well.

Virtuoso Playing Cards

Is the first 2 playing cards in our list too generic for you? then this
one might do the trick!

Playing cards that was made by a group of pro cardist with the
purpose of cardistry. extraordinary front and back design. each
card is unbelievably thin and manufactured by USPCC for those
sweet air cushion techs already break-in from the factory for the
best handle. this one costs a bit more and runs in limited stock so
it is a bit hard to find but if you are a hardcore cardist you want
this card in your collection.
