History of Cardistry

The word "Cardistry" is a combination of the words "card" and "artistry". when card tricks became popular in the 19th century, magicians would do card flourishes to demonstrate their skills. Cardistry at its core is not magic, and does not involve any sleight of hand, but rather uses manual dexterity, and tries to create a beautiful display through the movement of individual playing cards or the entire deck.
