Let's Learn your first Cardistry Move!

There are roughly 5 types of
Cardistry move

One-hand cut

A move that only requires
one hand to perform.

Two-hand cut

A move that requires both
hands to perform.

Isolation/Twirls

A move that can be done
with a single card.

Fan/Spread

A move that involves
spreading a deck of cards
in various ways

Ariel

A move that involves
shooting or throwing a
pocket or card in the air

beginners, Try them out Below!
